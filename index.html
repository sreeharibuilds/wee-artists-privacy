🎨 Privacy Policy for Wee Artists

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Wee Artists is a kids drawing application designed to provide a safe and creative environment for children. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information from users.

  • No login or account is required
  • No personal data is collected
  • No data is stored on external servers

All drawings are stored locally on the device.

3. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Tracking tools

5. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected, there is minimal risk to user privacy. All data remains on the user's device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
