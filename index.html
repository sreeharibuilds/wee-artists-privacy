👋 About Wee Artists

Wee Artists is a creative drawing app made for young children to explore imagination in a safe, joyful space. We built it with one principle above everything else: children deserve a private, ad-free place to create.

This Privacy Policy explains clearly what we do — and don't do — with your information.

🔒 Information We Collect

We collect absolutely nothing. No account or login is needed. Wee Artists does not collect:

✕
Names or emails
✕
Device identifiers
✕
Location data
✕
Usage analytics
✕
Drawings or content
✕
Children's data

📱 How Data Is Stored

All app data and artwork lives only on your device. Nothing is ever uploaded or transmitted.

📱
Local only
Drawings stay on your device exclusively
🚫
No cloud sync
Nothing is uploaded to any server
✈️
Works offline
No internet connection needed at any time

🧒 Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

Wee Artists is specifically built for young children. We fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and GDPR provisions for children's data protection.

Because we collect no data whatsoever, there is nothing to disclose, share, or delete. Parents and guardians can be confident that using Wee Artists leaves no digital footprint.

🚫 No Third-Party Services

Wee Artists does not integrate with any third-party services. There are no:

  • Advertisements or ad networks of any kind
  • Analytics or tracking tools (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel, Google Analytics)
  • Social media integrations or sharing features
  • In-app purchases linked to external payment services

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We will never introduce data collection without clearly updating this policy and seeking consent where required.
